Schmetterling

One of the oldest designs, the schmetterlings provide mobile homes that an entire family could live on while chasing after the temperate climate. A large cargo hold affords tons of cargo space, and a wide open deck provides enough room for nomads to stretch their legs and build freely. The heavy weight of the Schmetterling makes it less maneuverable.
