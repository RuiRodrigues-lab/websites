Firefly

The Firefly Walker is the first walker nomads build on their journey. Each nomad is instructed to build the walker early on in the tutorial. The Firefly walker is one of only 3 walkers that can operate without wings, using pedal power. The other 2 are both variants of the Spider Walker. The Firefly is unique from those two in that it can have wings added to it to increase its speed and acceleration. It is also unique in that it does not require the builder to be under the influence of Vision Powder to place down the blueprint.
