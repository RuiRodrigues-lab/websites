Spider

"A pedal-powered walker most nomads build when they want to begin their journey. Boasts a spacious inventory and can pack up to three crafting stations or small chests." This is an excellent walker for solo players that seek to explore hard areas. It's small size and maneuverability allows it to weave in and out of rocks/trees giving you a chance to escape much larger walkers as they are slowed by running into such objects. The spider walker is also capable of climbing up and down steep inclines as it sticks to the side of mountains. This makes it the ideal walker to explore deep ravines and high mountains. Despite its small size, and contrary to popular rumor, you can't pack the spider into other walkers.
