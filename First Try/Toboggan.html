Toboggan

The standard merchant's vessel built for outrunning pirates and getting the goods where they need to go. There's not much room for weapons, so this walker is usually accompanied by at least one battle-capable walker on its transport runs. The Cargo slots in the Toboggan stack resources to 999 instead of the usual 100 making it a great choice for resource gathering runs.
